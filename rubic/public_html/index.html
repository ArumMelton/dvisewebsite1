3 EPs (Stardust, Alpha, and Atmos)

Whole Lotta' Tracks

First and foremost, these EPs are very special to me. I have written/produced these tracks in the last 2 years while living my ordinary life. Full transparency, my music journey has been anything but ordinary. I have been dedicated to learning and creating, while *attempting* to support myself in a typical manner. Even while struggling for time, these tracks were some of the ones that poured out of me. I love to view electronic music as the ultimate release of emotion, and I believe these tracks do just that. I am planning to release Stardust, then Atmos, followed by Alpha.

Stardust is journey that is based on an imaginative story. I began producing for EP based on stories that I imagine last year. This story is about an astronaut that is traveling through space/time. The astronaut realizes that things aren't behaving normally as he enters the scope of a new galaxy.
